What is Vibe Battle?

The "Vibe Battle" is a 20x20 competition showcasing AI-assisted coding versus traditional coding. Non-technical Vibe Coders using AI tools compete against experienced coders developing without AI. The secret ingredient? Vibe Coding with Windsurf!

Competition Structure

Teams: 20 non-technical Vibe Coders using AI VS 20 experienced coders without AI.
Challenge: Create a fully functional web application based on a predefined business idea within the time limit.

Event Schedule

6:00 PM - Registration
6:15 PM - Rules Briefing
7:00 PM - Competition (60 min)
8:00 PM - Submission Deadline
9:15 PM - Awards Ceremony

Judging Criteria

Submissions scored 0-300 points based on: Functionality, Innovation/Creativity, Design & UX, and Completion & Speed. Highest score wins!
